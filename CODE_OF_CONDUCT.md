# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in OpenFrame respectful and harassment-free for everyone.

## Standards

Positive behavior includes:

- being respectful and constructive
- accepting feedback gracefully
- focusing on what is best for the project and community

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- personal attacks, trolling, or intimidation
- publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may take action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Reporting

Please report concerns privately to the maintainers through repository contact channels.
